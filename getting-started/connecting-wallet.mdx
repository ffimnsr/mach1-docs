---
title: Connecting Your Wallet
---

Once your wallet has been approved on the [whitelist](/getting-started/whitelist), you can connect it to MACH1 Markets. This gives the app permission to read your address and prompt you to sign transactions — no funds move during the connection itself.

## What you will need

- A whitelisted Ethereum-compatible wallet. Supported wallets include `MetaMask`, `Brave Wallet`, and `Phantom` (Ethereum mode).
- Your wallet browser extension installed and unlocked.

## Step 1: Open the app

Go to [app.m1.markets](https://app.m1.markets/en). You will see the main trading screen. In the top-right corner there is a **Connect Wallet** button.

## Step 2: Click Connect Wallet

Click the **Connect Wallet** button. A wallet picker modal will appear listing all the wallet providers the app supports.

## Step 3: Choose your wallet

Select the wallet you used when applying for the whitelist (e.g. MetaMask). The app will send a connection request to your browser extension.

> If you do not see your wallet listed, make sure the extension is installed and enabled in your browser.

## Step 4: Approve the connection in your wallet

Your wallet extension will open and ask you to connect the site. Click **Connect** (or **Approve**) to allow `app.m1.markets` to see your wallet address.

## Step 5: Sign the authentication message

![Wallet signature request](/images/wallet_signature_request.png)

After connecting, MACH1 will ask you to **sign a message** to verify ownership. This is a standard DEX authentication step — it does not cost gas and does not move any funds.

The signature prompt will look like the screenshot above. Click **Sign** to complete the process.

## Step 6: You are in

Once signed, your wallet address will appear in the top navigation bar and the app will unlock trading, deposits, and account features for your address.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to try |
|---|---|
| Signature window does not appear | Make sure your wallet extension is unlocked and the site has permission to connect. |
| Wrong wallet connected | Disconnect and reconnect, selecting the correct wallet. |
| "Not whitelisted" error | Confirm you submitted the correct address at `whitelist.0xmonaco.com` and that your application was approved. |
| Signature was declined | Click **Connect Wallet** again and accept the signature prompt to retry. |

## Next step

With your wallet connected, you are ready to fund your account. Head to **[Deposit USDC](/screens/deposit)** to add funds before trading.
