---
title: Whitelist Your Wallet
---

MACH1 is currently in early access. Before you can trade, you need to request an invitation and get your wallet address approved.

## What you will need

- An Ethereum-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Brave Wallet, Phantom, etc.) that you plan to use for trading.
- A Twitter account and optionally a Telegram account for verification.
- Your wallet address (the public `0x...` address, **never** your private key or seed phrase).

## Step 1: Go to the whitelist form

Open [whitelist.0xmonaco.com](https://whitelist.0xmonaco.com) in your browser.

![Whitelist request form](/images/whitelist.0xmonaco.com_.png)

You will see the Monaco access request form with a set of required fields and a **Request Access** button at the bottom.

## Step 2: Fill in your details

| Field | Required | What to enter |
|---|---|---|
| Wallet Address | Yes | Your `0x...` wallet address |
| Email Address | Yes | Email where you want to receive approval updates |
| Twitter Username | Yes | Your public Twitter / X handle |
| Telegram Username | No | Your Telegram handle for additional contact |

> **Double-check your wallet address.** Once submitted, approvals are linked to the address you provide. If you use the wrong address you will need to reapply.

## Step 3: Submit the form

Click **Request Access**. Your application is sent to the MACH1 team for review. You will be notified at the email address you provided once your wallet is approved.

## What happens next

Approval can take some time. While you wait:
- Make sure your wallet extension (MetaMask, etc.) is installed and set up.
- Read the [Connecting Your Wallet](/getting-started/connecting-wallet) page so you know what to expect after approval.

## Tips

- Use the same wallet address you intend to trade with. You cannot change it after submission without re-applying.
- Submitting the form indicates you agree to receive onboarding updates from MACH1.
- Do not share your private key or seed phrase with anyone, including MACH1 support.
