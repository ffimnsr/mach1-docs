Last updated: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MACH1 may collect, use, and process information when you access or use `m1.markets` and related services (the `Website`).

## 1. Data we may collect

We may collect:

1. wallet-related public identifiers and transaction metadata;
2. technical and usage data, such as device type, browser details, pages visited, request timing, and IP-based region signals;
3. communication data when you contact support, including your email and message content.

## 2. How we use data

We may use collected data to:

1. operate and secure the Website;
2. provide core functionality and troubleshoot issues;
3. detect abuse, fraud, and policy violations;
4. improve reliability, performance, and user experience;
5. comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Cookies and analytics

MACH1 may use strictly necessary cookies and limited analytics tooling to maintain service quality and security. These tools are not used to sell your personal data.

## 4. Third-party services

Some functions depend on third-party providers (for example, wallet providers, infrastructure vendors, or analytics and security services). Their processing is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

## 5. Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including legal, security, compliance, and operational requirements.

## 6. Data sharing

We may share data with service providers and professional advisors where required to operate the Website, enforce policies, comply with law, or protect users and platform integrity.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and to request portability where applicable.

To make a privacy request, contact `support@m1.markets`.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's data

The Website is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can review and delete data where appropriate.

## 10. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material updates will be reflected by the `Last updated` date.

## 11. Contact

Questions about privacy or data handling can be sent to `support@m1.markets`.
