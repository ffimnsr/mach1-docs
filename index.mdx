MACH1 Markets (`app.m1.markets`) is a decentralised exchange for spot and perpetuals trading. It supports real-world assets alongside crypto pairs, all settled in USDC on the Sei network.

This documentation will guide you through every part of the platform — from getting access to placing your first trade.

## Quick start path

If you are completely new, follow these steps in order:

1. **[Whitelist your wallet](/getting-started/whitelist)** — Request access at `whitelist.0xmonaco.com`.
2. **[Connect your wallet](/getting-started/connecting-wallet)** — Link your approved wallet to the app.
3. **[Deposit USDC](/screens/deposit)** — Fund your trading account.
4. **[Place a trade](/screens/trade)** — Use the main trading screen to buy or sell.
5. **[Track your account](/screens/portfolio)** — Monitor balances, positions and PnL.

## Explore the platform

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| [Trade](/screens/trade) | Live orderbook with market and limit orders across spot and perp markets. |
| [Swap](/screens/swap) | Instantly exchange two tokens at the best available rate. |
| [Screener](/screens/screener) | Scan all listed markets sorted by volume, price change, and more. |
| [Portfolio](/screens/portfolio) | View your balances, open positions, PnL, and full trade history. |
| [Leaderboard](/screens/leaderboard) | Compare your performance against other traders. |
