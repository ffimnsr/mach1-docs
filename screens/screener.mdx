The Screener gives you a bird’s-eye view of all markets listed on MACH1 in a single table. Use it to spot trending assets, compare price movements, and find trading opportunities — then jump straight into the order book with one click.

![Screener screen](/images/screen-screener.png)

## How to open the Screener

Click **Screener** in the top navigation bar, or go to `app.m1.markets/en/screener`.

## Understanding the market table

The screener lists every market with the following columns:

| Column | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Market | Token pair name and symbol (e.g. `BTC/USDC`) |
| Price | Current last traded price |
| 24h Volume | Total notional volume traded in the last 24 hours |
| 24h Change | Percentage price change over the last 24 hours (green = up, red = down) |

Example markets visible on MACH1 include `BTC/USDC`, `ETH/USDC`, `SOL/USDC`, `SPX/USDC`, `META/USDC`, and `TSLA/USDC`.

## Filtering by category

At the top of the table there are category tabs:

- **CRYPTO** — Cryptocurrency pairs like BTC, ETH, SOL, HYPE.
- **EQUITIES** — Real-world equity-linked pairs like SPX, META, TSLA, NVDA, AMZN.
- **COOKING** — Meme and community tokens like FARTCOIN.

Click a category tab to filter the list to that group only. Click the tab again or select a different one to switch.

## Sorting the table

Click any column header to sort the table by that field. Click it again to reverse the sort order. For example:
- Click **24h Volume** to find the most actively traded markets.
- Click **24h Change** to see the biggest movers of the day (sort descending for top gainers, ascending for biggest decliners).

## Searching for a specific market

Use the search bar at the top of the table to filter by token name or symbol. Start typing (e.g. `ETH`) and the list will narrow in real time.

## Opening a market to trade

Click any row in the screener table to open that market on the full [Trade screen](/screens/trade). The chart, order book, and order entry panel will all switch to that pair.

## Tips

- Sort by **24h Volume** descending to find the most liquid markets with tighter spreads.
- Use the **24h Change** sort to quickly spot the biggest gainers and losers of the day.
- The screener updates live — you do not need to refresh the page to see the latest data.
