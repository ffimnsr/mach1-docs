**What you are seeing**
The market screener route (`/en/screener`) for scanning instruments and comparing market activity from one table view.

**Key regions**
- Search and filter controls at the top of the table.
- Sortable market columns (for example price, change, and volume fields).
- Scrollable result set for quickly moving across listed markets.

**Notable behaviors**
- Sorting and filtering update the visible set without leaving the page.
- Clicking a market row is the fastest path back to focused trading on that instrument.
