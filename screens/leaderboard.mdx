title: Leaderboard
---

The Leaderboard ranks all traders on MACH1 by performance. Use it to benchmark your results against other users, discover top traders, and track your own ranking over time.

![Leaderboard screen](/images/screen-leaderboard.png)

## How to open the Leaderboard

Click **Leaderboard** in the top navigation bar.

## Reading the leaderboard table

Each row represents one wallet address and shows performance metrics for the current ranking period. Typical columns include:

| Column | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Rank | Ordinal position from 1st upwards |
| Address | The trader’s wallet address (abbreviated) |
| PnL | Profit and loss for the ranking period |
| Volume | Total notional volume traded |
| Win Rate | Percentage of trades that were profitable |

## Sorting and filtering

- Click any column header to sort by that metric.
- Use the **search** bar to find a specific wallet address by pasting it in.
- Use the **pagination controls** at the bottom to navigate through all ranked wallets.
- Use the **results-per-page** selector to show more or fewer rows at once.

## Finding your own rank

1. Copy your wallet address from the top navigation bar.
2. Paste it into the leaderboard search bar.
3. Your row will appear with your current rank and statistics.

## Tips

- Rankings are calculated over a specific window (the active competition period). Check the label above the table for the current window dates.
- Leaderboard positions update periodically as new trades are settled — your rank may shift throughout the day.
- Looking at top-ranked wallets can give insight into which markets are generating the most activity.
