**What you are seeing**
The leaderboard route (`/en/leaderboard`) ranking wallets by selected performance metrics.

**Key regions**
- Ranking table with address and metric columns.
- Controls for search, pagination, and result sizing.
- Performance context labels to clarify the ranking window.

**Notable behaviors**
- Sorting changes ranking order directly in the table.
- Wallet rows provide quick discovery for top-performing accounts.
