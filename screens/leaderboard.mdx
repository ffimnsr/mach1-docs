**What you are seeing**
The leaderboard view ranking top traders over a `30D` window. It highlights account value, PNL, ROI, and volume.

**Key regions**
- Filter controls for the time window and search by wallet address.
- Leaderboard table with rank, trader address, and performance metrics.
- Pagination controls and rows-per-page selector.

**Notable behaviors**
- ROI is derived from PNL relative to starting account value for the window.
- The table excludes small accounts per the note at the bottom of the panel.
