---
title: Withdraw USDC
---

Withdrawing moves USDC from your MACH1 trading account back to your connected Sei wallet. You can only withdraw funds that are not currently locked as margin in open positions.

## Before you start

- Your wallet must be [connected](/getting-started/connecting-wallet).
- You must have available (non-margined) USDC in your trading account.
- Close or reduce any positions that are using the balance you want to withdraw.

## How to withdraw

### Step 1: Open the Withdraw modal

From the [Portfolio](/screens/portfolio) screen or the account panel on the trading screen, click the **Withdraw** button. The **Withdraw USDC to Sei** modal will open.

![Withdraw modal](/images/modal-withdraw.png)

### Step 2: Check your available balance

The modal shows your **Available** balance — this is the amount you can withdraw right now. It excludes any USDC currently locked as collateral in open positions.

### Step 3: Enter the amount

Type the amount you want to withdraw. You can enter any amount up to and including the displayed available balance.

> If you want to withdraw everything, type the full available balance shown, or use the **MAX** shortcut if present.

### Step 4: Confirm the withdrawal

Click **Withdraw** to submit. Your wallet will prompt you to approve the transaction. Once the transaction is confirmed on-chain, the USDC will appear back in your Sei wallet.

## Tips

- Withdrawals are on-chain transactions and require a small SEI gas fee.
- You cannot withdraw funds that are currently collateralising an open position. Close the position first, then withdraw.
- If the Withdraw button is greyed out, check that you have entered a valid amount that does not exceed your available balance.
- After withdrawing, verify the funds arrived in your wallet by checking your wallet balance or block explorer.
