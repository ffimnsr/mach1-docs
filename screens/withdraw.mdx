![Withdraw modal](/images/modal-withdraw.png)

**What you are seeing**
The `Withdraw USDC to Sei` modal used to move funds out of the trading account.

**Key regions**
- Asset selector (shown as `USDC`).
- Amount input with an `Available` balance helper.
- Action row with cancel and submit.

**Notable behaviors**
- Withdraw action is disabled until amount validation passes.
- Availability shown in the modal reflects current account state.
