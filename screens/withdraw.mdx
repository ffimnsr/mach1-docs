**What you are seeing**
A withdrawal modal for sending `USDC` from the MACH1 account to the Sei network. It sits above the trading view so users can confirm amounts in context.

**Key regions**
- Asset selector showing `USDC`.
- Amount input with total and available balance.
- Primary `Withdraw` action and a secondary cancel action.

**Notable behaviors**
- The modal validates the requested amount against available balance.
- The underlying trade screen remains visible but inactive.
