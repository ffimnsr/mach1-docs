**What you are seeing**
The portfolio route (`/en/portfolio`) that aggregates balances, positions, and account-level performance views.

**Key regions**
- Portfolio summary section for high-level account metrics.
- Holdings and activity tables for assets and account events.
- Utility actions for managing funds and reviewing performance context.

**Notable behaviors**
- Data views update with current account and network context.
- Tables can be used to drill from summary state into specific assets or events.
