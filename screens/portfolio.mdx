The Portfolio screen is your account dashboard. It shows your overall performance metrics, current balances, open positions, and the full history of your activity on MACH1.

![Portfolio screen](/images/screen-portfolio.png)

## How to open Portfolio

Click **Portfolio** in the top navigation bar, or go to `app.m1.markets/en/portfolio`.

## Account summary

At the top of the Portfolio screen you will see your high-level account metrics:

| Metric | What it means |
|---|---|
| **14 Day Volume** | Your total notional trading volume over the last 14 days. This determines your fee tier — higher volume = lower fees. |
| **Fees (Taker / Maker)** | Your current fee rates for taking liquidity (market orders) and making liquidity (limit orders). Click **View Fee Schedule** to see all tiers. |
| **30D PnL** | Your realised and unrealised profit and loss over the past 30 days. |
| **Volume** | Total volume across all time or the selected period. |
| **Max Drawdown** | The largest peak-to-trough decline in your account equity. A useful risk metric. |
| **Total Equity** | The current total value of your account (spot + perps). |
| **Spot Account Equity** | The value of your spot holdings only. |
| **Perps Equity** | The value locked in open perpetuals positions. |

## Account tabs

Below the summary, the account panel has multiple tabs:

### Balances
Shows all tokens held in your trading account and their current values. Use this to confirm a deposit arrived or to see what is available to trade.

### Positions
Lists all currently open perpetuals positions, including:
- Entry price and current mark price
- Unrealised PnL
- Liquidation price — the price at which your position would be force-closed
- Leverage and margin used

From this tab you can also partially or fully close a position.

### Open Orders
Shows all limit orders that are active in the order book. You can cancel individual orders or all orders at once from here.

### Order History
A complete history of all orders you have ever placed, with status (filled, cancelled, rejected).

### Trade History
Every individual fill with the exact price, size, fee, and timestamp.

### Deposit / Withdrawal History
A log of all your [deposits](/screens/deposit) and [withdrawals](/screens/withdraw).

## Managing funds from Portfolio

- Click **Deposit** to add more USDC to your account (see [Deposit USDC](/screens/deposit)).
- Click **Withdraw** to move USDC back to your wallet (see [Withdraw USDC](/screens/withdraw)).

## Tips

- Review your **Max Drawdown** and **30D PnL** regularly to keep a realistic picture of your performance.
- Monitor your **14 Day Volume** as it approaches the next fee tier to time larger trades.
- If a deposit is not showing in Balances, wait a few seconds and refresh. On-chain confirmations can occasionally be delayed.
