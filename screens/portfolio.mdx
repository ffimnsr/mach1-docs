**What you are seeing**
The portfolio overview with performance summaries, fee tiers, and account activity tabs. This view aggregates spot and perpetuals metrics for the selected period.

**Key regions**
- Summary cards for volume, fees, and equity metrics.
- Performance chart toggle between `Volume` and `PNL`.
- Action buttons for `Deposit` and `Withdraw`.
- Tabbed history section for balances, positions, orders, and credit events.

**Notable behaviors**
- The timeframe selector (e.g., `30D`) updates the performance metrics and chart.
- Tabs in the lower panel switch between different account datasets.
