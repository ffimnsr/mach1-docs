title: Request Test Tokens
---

If you are using MACH1 on testnet, you will need test USDC before you can trade. The faucet lets you request a free batch of test tokens to get started without using real funds.

> **Testnet only.** The faucet is not available on mainnet. If you are on mainnet, fund your account by [depositing real USDC](/screens/deposit) from your wallet.

## Before you start

- Your wallet must be [connected](/getting-started/connecting-wallet).
- You must be on the testnet environment.

## How to request test tokens

### Step 1: Open the faucet

In the app, look for the **Request Test Tokens** option. This is typically accessible from the account panel or a settings area when on testnet. The faucet modal will open.

![Faucet request modal](/images/modal-faucet-request.png)

### Step 2: Review the request notice

The modal shows information about the faucet, including the **request cadence** — how often you are allowed to request tokens. The standard cadence is once every **24 hours**.

If you have already requested tokens within the last 24 hours, the **Request** button will be disabled until the cooldown period expires.

### Step 3: Click Request

Click the **Request** button to claim your test tokens. The faucet will send a batch of test USDC to your connected wallet address.

### Step 4: Confirm the tokens arrived

After a moment, go to the [Portfolio](/screens/portfolio) screen and check the **Balances** tab. Your test USDC balance should now reflect the faucet amount.

Once funded, you can [deposit](/screens/deposit) the test USDC into your trading account and start exploring the trading features.

## Tips

- The faucet is rate-limited to prevent abuse. You can request once per 24-hour window per wallet address.
- If the Request button remains disabled and you believe you are past the cooldown, try refreshing the page and reconnecting your wallet.
- Test tokens have no real-world value and cannot be withdrawn to mainnet.
