title: Notification Center

The Notification Center lets you control which alerts MACH1 sends to you. You can enable or disable notifications by category, bulk-pause all alerts temporarily, or turn off notifications entirely.

![Notification center modal](/images/modal-notification-center.png)

## How to open the Notification Center

Click the **Settings** icon in the top-right navigation and select **Notification Center**, or look for the bell icon if present in the navigation bar.

## Understanding the modal

The modal is divided into:

1. **Alert delivery summary** — shows how many categories are currently active and provides two quick actions:
   - **Enable all** — turns on every notification category at once.
   - **Pause all** — temporarily mutes all notifications without disabling them permanently.

2. **Category toggles** — individual switches for each notification type.

3. **Footer** — a global disable option and a close button.

## Notification categories

| Category | What triggers it |
|---|---|
| **Fills** | When one of your orders is matched and filled |
| **Momentum triggers** | When a market you are watching hits a significant price movement |
| **Portfolio health** | Alerts about your overall account risk, margin levels, or drawdown |
| **Wallet security** | Unusual activity or important events linked to your connected wallet |

## How to manage your notifications

### Enable or disable a specific category

1. Open the Notification Center.
2. Find the category you want to change.
3. Click the toggle next to it. Green = enabled, grey = disabled.
4. Changes apply immediately — there is no separate Save button for individual toggles.

### Pause all notifications temporarily

1. Open the Notification Center.
2. Click **Pause all**. All categories will be muted until you re-enable them.
3. To resume, click **Enable all**.

### Turn off all notifications permanently

1. Open the Notification Center.
2. Click the global **Disable** option in the footer.

## Tips

- The **Fills** category is recommended to stay enabled so you always know when your orders execute.
- **Portfolio health** alerts can give early warning of approaching liquidation levels— keep these on if you hold perp positions.
- The modal is an overlay. The trade screen behind it is read-only while the modal is open.
