![Notification center modal](/images/modal-notification-center.png)

**What you are seeing**
The `Notification Center` modal opened from `Settings`, with alert categories and bulk controls.

**Key regions**
- Alert delivery summary with `Enable all` and `Pause all` actions.
- Category toggles such as fills, momentum triggers, portfolio health, and wallet security.
- Footer actions including global disable and close.

**Notable behaviors**
- Each category can be toggled independently.
- The modal is an overlay and keeps the underlying trade screen read-only while open.
