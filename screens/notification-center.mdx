![Notification center modal](/images/app.m1.markets_en%20%286%29.png)

**What you are seeing**
A modal overlay for configuring alert delivery and notification categories. This screen lets users opt in to different alert types while the backend is being finalized.

**Key regions**
- Modal header with explanation of the notification system.
- Toggle list for alert categories such as order fills, price momentum, and announcements.
- Global controls for enabling, pausing, or disabling everything.

**Notable behaviors**
- Categories can be enabled independently; checkboxes indicate active alerts.
- The modal blocks the trading view to keep attention on preferences.
