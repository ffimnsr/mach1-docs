title: Swap Screen
---

The Swap screen lets you instantly exchange one supported token for another. Unlike placing a trade on the order book, a swap gives you a quoted rate upfront and executes in one step — no order management needed.

![Swap screen](/images/screen-swap.png)

## When to use Swap vs Trade

| Use Swap when… | Use Trade when… |
|---|---|
| You want a simple one-click token exchange | You need a specific limit price |
| You do not want to deal with the order book | You are managing a larger position with detailed entries |
| The amount is smaller and slippage is acceptable | You want partial fills or advanced order types |

## How to swap tokens

### Step 1: Open the Swap screen

Click **Swap** in the top navigation bar, or go directly to `app.m1.markets/en/swap`.

### Step 2: Select the token you want to pay with (YOU PAY)

1. In the **YOU PAY** field, click the token selector (defaults to `BTC`).
2. A token search modal opens. You can search by symbol or paste a contract address.
3. Select your token. Your available balance for that token is shown below the field.

### Step 3: Select the token you want to receive (YOU RECEIVE)

1. In the **YOU RECEIVE** field, click the token selector (defaults to `USDC`).
2. Search and select the destination token.

### Step 4: Enter the amount

Type the amount in the **YOU PAY** field. The app will calculate and display the estimated amount you will receive based on the live rate.

The info panel below the inputs shows:
- **Rate** — the current exchange rate between the two tokens (e.g. `1 BTC ~ 71,611 USDC`).
- **Slippage tolerance** — the maximum price movement you accept before the swap is cancelled (default `0.50%`).
- **Estimated output** — the amount you should receive after fees.
- **Quote status** — shows `Ready` when a valid quote is available.

### Step 5: Review the swap route

The **Swap Route** section shows how the trade will be executed:
- **Route type** — `Direct` means the swap goes straight through a single pool.
- **Number of hops** — how many pools the route passes through (1 hop = direct).

A direct single-hop route is generally the cheapest and fastest path.

### Step 6: Connect wallet and execute

If your wallet is not connected, click **Connect wallet to swap**. Once connected, the button changes to **Swap**. Click it to execute. Your wallet will prompt you to approve the transaction.

## Tips

- Quotes refresh automatically as the market moves. If the rate changes significantly before you confirm, the quote will update.
- If you are swapping a large amount, check the slippage tolerance — you may want to reduce it to avoid an unfavourable fill.
- If no direct route exists between two tokens, the app will route through intermediate pools automatically.
