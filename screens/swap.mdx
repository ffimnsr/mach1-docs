**What you are seeing**
The swap interface for quickly exchanging assets at live market rates. The example shows swapping `BTC` to `USDC` with a direct route.

**Key regions**
- Left card for swap inputs, including `You Pay` and `You Receive` selectors and balances.
- Rate and slippage tolerance summary with quote status.
- Right card for the swap route, asset path, and hop count.

**Notable behaviors**
- The route panel confirms whether the swap is direct or routed.
- A wallet connection is required to enable the swap action.
