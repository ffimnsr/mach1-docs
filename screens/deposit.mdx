**What you are seeing**
A deposit modal for bringing `USDC` into the MACH1 account from Sei. It captures the amount and shows the maximum available balance.

**Key regions**
- Asset selector locked to `USDC` in this example.
- Amount input with a displayed `Max` balance.
- Primary `Deposit` action and a cancel action.

**Notable behaviors**
- The deposit button is disabled until a valid amount is entered.
- The modal confirms the asset and network context before submission.
