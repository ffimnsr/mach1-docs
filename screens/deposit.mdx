![Deposit modal](/images/modal-deposit.png)

**What you are seeing**
The `Deposit USDC from Sei` modal used to move funds into the trading account.

**Key regions**
- Asset selector (shown as `USDC`).
- Amount input with a quick `MAX` shortcut.
- Action row with cancel and submit.

**Notable behaviors**
- Deposit action is disabled until a valid amount is entered.
- The modal is scoped to the currently selected network context.
