![Language menu](/images/modal-language-menu.png)

**What you are seeing**
The language dropdown opened from the globe control in the top-right navigation.

**Key regions**
- Locale list with language labels and flags.
- Active language indicator on the currently selected option.

**Notable behaviors**
- Language selection applies to the current app session view.
- The menu opens in place without navigating away from the active route.
