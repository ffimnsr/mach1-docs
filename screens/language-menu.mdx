---
title: Language Menu
---

MACH1 supports multiple interface languages. The Language Menu lets you switch the app’s display language at any time without navigating away from your current screen.

![Language menu](/images/modal-language-menu.png)

## How to open the Language Menu

Look for the **globe icon** (🌐) in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. Click it to open the language dropdown.

## How to change the language

1. Click the globe icon in the top-right navigation.
2. The language menu will open, showing a list of available languages with their flags and names.
3. The currently active language is highlighted with an indicator.
4. Click any language in the list to switch to it. The app interface will update immediately to the selected language.

## Supported languages

The language list in the menu shows all currently available locales. Common options include English and several other regional languages. The exact list may grow as MACH1 adds more translations.

## Tips

- Language changes apply to your current session view and are remembered for future visits.
- Switching language does not navigate you away from the page you are on — the menu closes in place.
- If you see untranslated text after switching, some newer UI sections may not yet have a full translation. English will be used as a fallback.
