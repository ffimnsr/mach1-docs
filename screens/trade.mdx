**What you are seeing**
The primary trading workspace for the `BTC/USDC` spot market. It combines a price chart, order book, trade ticket, and account panels so users can analyze, place orders, and monitor balances in one view.

**Key regions**
- Top navigation and market ribbon for moving between app sections and scanning market tickers.
- Chart area with timeframes, indicators, and volume bars for technical analysis.
- Order book and recent orders panel to the right of the chart for real-time depth.
- Order ticket on the far right with `Market`/`Limit`/`Pro` tabs, buy/sell toggle, size slider, and time-in-force.
- Bottom account area for balances, positions, and order history.

**Notable behaviors**
- The buy/sell ticket highlights available balance, fees, and estimated execution impact.
- The order book is split into asks (red) and bids (green) with a spread row.
