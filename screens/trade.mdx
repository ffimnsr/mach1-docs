**What you are seeing**
The main trading workspace on `app.m1.markets/en`, centered on a selected spot market with live price data.

**Key regions**
- Top navigation for `Trade`, `Screener`, `Swap`, `Portfolio`, and `Leaderboard`.
- Live market ticker ribbon and market selector.
- TradingView chart panel with timeframe and drawing controls.
- Order book and order entry panel with `Market` and `Limit` tabs.
- Account summary and history tabs (`Balances`, `Positions`, `Open Orders`, and more).

**Notable behaviors**
- Order actions are gated by wallet state and available balance.
- Market stats, chart, and order book update continuously from live feeds.
